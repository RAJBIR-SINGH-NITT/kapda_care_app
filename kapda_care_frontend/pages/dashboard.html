Fabric Care FAQ
How to care for silk sarees? ▾
Silk sarees should only be dry-cleaned or hand-washed in cold water with pH-neutral soap. Never wring or tumble dry. Store in muslin cloth away from sunlight. Our Heritage Care uses exactly this process.
Remove curry stains from cotton? ▾
Act fast — rinse with cold water, apply dish soap with baking soda paste, let sit 15 minutes. Wash in warm water. For stubborn stains, our enzyme-based Wash & Care pre-treatment is highly effective.
How often to dry-clean a suit? ▾
No more than 2–3 times per year — over-cleaning weakens fibres. Between cleans, use a garment brush and steam iron. Silver & Gold members get quarterly suit care at no extra cost.
What is Heritage Care? ▾
Heritage Care is our premium manual service for heirloom fabrics — Banarasi sarees, embroidered sherwanis, vintage pieces. Hand-washed with soft water and pH-balanced detergent, then air-dried in shade. No machines ever touch these garments.
